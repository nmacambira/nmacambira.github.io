ShakeTheBag is a great tool for teachers, families, or anyone who wants to randomly select names, numbers, images, dices or answers!

We have a crystal ball to help with the difficult questions of life, although it is not very reliable! :P

It does not matter if you are at work, at school, at the gym or at the family barbecue. ShakeTheBag will randomly pick a name / number / image / dice / answer for you.

Features:

  • - Random Names;
  • - Random Numbers;
  • - Random Images;
  • - Random Dices;
  • - Random Answers (Crystal Ball);
  • - Animated Graphics make the whole experience a pleasure.
